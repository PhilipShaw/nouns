What's the point of this site?

Nouns is a simple web app that allows users to guess how much another person admires something on a 1-10 scale. After a user creates content for someone else, the recipient can rate that content.

Each unit of content is called a "noun," because most content will be based on a person, place, thing or an activity (also called a gerund). Below is an example noun.



Rapport is earned after the recipient rates your noun. The more accurate a noun creator's guess is, the more a user's rapport increases. A perfect guess is worth three points.
